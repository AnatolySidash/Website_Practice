What is Stroke?

A stroke is a sudden interruption in the blood supply of the brain. An abrupt blockage of the arteries leads to the brain (ischemic stroke) or bleeding into brain tissue when a blood vessel ruptures (hemorrhagic stroke). A stroke is a medical emergency. It is time to make action immediately.
