Why Us

GHG Emissions industry is generally aimed at big polluters whose processes will be complex, but process is actually much simpler for majority of small and services-based businesses. Industry is also generally dominated by consulting oriented businesses who rely on maximising the complexity to maximise fees per engagement rather than scale.
We use the same process, same protocols and standards, but use software and process optimisation to provide a service which is about maximising customer value.

Household Emissions Calculator

Australian homes are responsible for a significant percentage of our total emissions, average estimates range from 15 to 20 tonnes per year per household which is one of the highest in the world, and makes up over 20% of the total emissions for Australia. With the recent shift to increased WFH, residential emissions are becoming an even more important factor. Our household emissions calculator will give you a good idea of your emissions and the most likely options for reductions.

Individual Commute Calculator

Another significant source of emissions is the daily commute to work which a large but decreasing percentage of the community still engages in.
